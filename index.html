About Pankaj Tambe

Biography

Pankaj Tambe completed his schooling in Sangmeshwar village and pursued Engineering in Ratnagiri. He played Tennis Ball Cricket in Maharashtra tournaments, earning awards like Man of the Series, Best Batsman, and Best Bowler.

Professional Journey

Completed Computer Engineering in Mumbai. Managed IT infrastructure & served as IT teacher at St. Teresa’s Convent Girls School. Handled IT infrastructure for TPC, AG Infra, Supreme, Inventys. Founder of Sparsh Infotech & Stuti Enterprise, providing IT, networking, hardware, and CCTV solutions with 12+ years of experience.
