Hardware Notes & Tips

  • Routers: Select by throughput & features — vpn/firewall, gigabit ports for office use.
  • Switches: Managed PoE switches are ideal for camera deployments; verify PoE watt budget.
  • Cameras: 2MP for general, 4MP+ for ID zones; use PTZ where wide area coverage is needed.
  • Storage: NAS with RAID for local backups + periodic cloud sync for disaster recovery.
  • Cabling: CAT6A recommended for future-proofing; STP where EMI/noise present.
