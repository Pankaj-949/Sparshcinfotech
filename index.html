Hardware Notes & Recommendations

Quick Buying Guide

  • Routers: For small offices, get dual-band gigabit routers with hardware NAT. For larger deployments, prefer enterprise routers with proper VPN/firewall capabilities.
  • Switches: Use Managed PoE switches for camera deployments. Check total PoE budget (watts) before deploying many cameras.
  • Cameras: Choose resolution based on need; 2MP for general surveillance, 4MP+ for identification zones. PTZ for wide areas.
  • Storage: Use NAS with RAID 1/5 for redundancy and keep an offsite/cloud copy for disaster recovery.
  • Cabling: Prefer CAT6A for future-proofing; use STP where heavy EMI exists.
